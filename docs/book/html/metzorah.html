Metzorah

  1. Can the metzorah be cleansed at night? Proof?

  2. The metzorah is put outside of the camp (or city). How does the Kohein see him?

  3. Can you bring an example from the mivtzoyim which is similar to the above?

  4. Why does the metzorah bring birds for his cleansing?

  5. The species of the birds is not mentioned. Which birds are not acceptable?

  6. Why does the metzorah bring the etz erez, shei, tolaas, ezov?

  7. What is done with the two birds (with the items from the previous question)?

  8. What does the metzorah do on the first day of his cleanliness? On the eighth day?

  9. On which parts of the metzorah’s body do they put the blood? The oil?

  10. From the ‘13 midos’ of Rabbi Yishmael (in Shacharis) we use three in this parsha. Which midos are used? How?

  11. Rashi says he doesn’t know why a certain thing is called by its name. Which?

  12. A metzorah who is poor, how are his korbanos different?

  13. Why is it good news that some homes in Eretz Yisroel have negayim?

  14. Why does the Torah command to empty the house before the Kohein comes?

  15. What is done to the house when the negah is first seen?

  16. What happens to the person who eats in or sleeps in or just enters the closed house?

  17. If the negah grows bigger on the seventh day, what is done to the house?

  18. What is done for the house after the ‘new stones’ remain clean?

  19. An item which a zav touches, sits on, or sleeps on, what tumah does it get?

  20. When does a zav become tahor? What does he have to bring?

  21. What kind of tumah does a lady zavah cause?

  22. What kind of taharah is common in (almost) all cases?
